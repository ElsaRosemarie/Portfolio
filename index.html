Hi!

Elsa (they/she) is a queer illustrator, graphic designer and anthropologist. Their work centres around the complexities of more-than-human entanglement, the search for bodily softness and the joys of the whimsical. With their eye for detail and their love for colours and textures, they create emotionally engaging publications, striking visual identities and poetic mixed-media paintings.
